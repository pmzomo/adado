Co-Founder & President

Dr. Philip Mwalali

Physician • OB-GYN Specialist • UN Advisor • Entrepreneur

Dr. Philip Mwalali is one of the five Kambas in the Diaspora who felt the need to have an organization that brings the Kambas in Diaspora together for development. He is a physician, an obstetrician gynecologist and reproductive health specialist with an entrepreneurial mind and a deep passion for community-based initiatives.

Clinical & International Career

Dr. Mwalali worked at Kenyatta National Hospital where he served as both a consultant and lecturer in Obstetrics and Gynecology at the University of Nairobi, before accepting a tour of service to the United Nations Systems — UNDP, UNFPA, and WHO.

Under his UN assignment as Technical Advisor, he provided technical assistance across multiple countries in Africa:

Botswana Zambia Lesotho South Africa Swaziland Malawi Zimbabwe Namibia The Gambia Mozambique

Despite his global career, Dr. Mwalali remained deeply connected to his community in Kilisa Village, Kiteta location, Makueni District. Upon return from his UN assignment, he set up the African Economic Foundation (africaeconomicfoundation.net), organized his village community and started providing guidance for community participation as a key strategy for sustainable development. Through initiatives such as subsurface dams for small-scale farming, Kilisa Village started transforming into a "Center of Excellence" in community-based initiatives.

Leadership & Ventures

  • ✭ Co-Founder & President — Akamba Diaspora Advancement & Development Organization — ADADO.ORG
  • ✭ Co-Founder — African Diaspora Coalition of USA — ADCUSA.ORG
  • ✭ Co-Founder — Diaspora University Town, Voi, Kenya — leading the development of the Hospital & health system — DUT.OR.KE
  • ✭ Founder — The Chariot Group LLC — enhancing development through guidance, entrepreneurship, and trade
  • ✭ Founder — African Economic Foundation — africaeconomicfoundation.net

As ADADO Co-Founder and President, Dr. Mwalali brings his extensive medical expertise, UN experience, and entrepreneurial vision to the organization. His experiences are instrumental in the development of Centers of Excellence — a model ADADO is keen on expanding elsewhere in Kenya and Africa at large.
