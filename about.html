Our Story

Born from a Shared
Kamba Identity

ADADO — the Akamba Diaspora Advancement & Development Organization, Inc. — was founded by passionate Kamba community members living in North Carolina who felt a deep calling to serve their homeland while strengthening their community abroad.

We recognized that the Kamba people, spread across the globe, carry with them an incredible wealth of skills, education, and resources. But without coordination and direction, this potential remained untapped. ADADO was created to be that bridge.

Operating under the principle that "we are all in this together," ADADO brings together professionals, farmers, educators, faith leaders, and community champions from the diaspora and Kenya to work toward a shared vision of prosperity for all Kamba people.

FOUNDING

ADADO Established in North Carolina

Kamba diaspora members in NC unite to form ADADO, Inc. as a formal non-profit organization focused on sustainable community development.

OUTREACH

First Town Halls & Community Forums

ADADO launches inaugural town hall meetings in North Carolina and establishes dialogue channels with communities in Machakos, Kitui, and Makueni.

PROGRAMS

Agriculture & Education Initiatives Launch

ADADO launches its flagship sustainable agriculture program and begins planning for Centers of Excellence in partnership with local government and NGOs.

FUTURE

Centers of Excellence — In Progress

Plans underway to establish the first ADADO Center of Excellence in Machakos County — a hub for agricultural training, vocational skills, and entrepreneurship.

Our Purpose

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To be a transformational force for the Kamba Community in North Carolina and Kenya, fostering sustainable development, educational excellence, and socio-economic growth through collaborative and innovative initiatives.

Our Mission

  • ✦ Promote sustainable agricultural practices and technology to improve food security.
  • ✦ Establish Centers of Excellence to train and uplift Kamba people for future generations.
  • ✦ Encourage civic engagement within the diaspora to leverage expertise for development.
  • ✦ Build faith-based coalitions to support foundation projects in U.S. and Kenya.
  • ✦ Facilitate inclusive dialogues through town halls for holistic community development.

Our People

The Resilient Kamba People

The Akamba people — known for their resilience, craftsmanship, and entrepreneurial spirit — are one of Kenya's most prominent ethnic groups. Historically settled in the semi-arid Eastern Province, the Kamba have developed remarkable strategies for survival in challenging environments.

Known as master wood carvers, traders, and warriors, the Kamba have always been outward-looking. Today, thousands of Kamba professionals and students live across the globe — in the U.S., UK, Canada, and beyond — carrying with them the values of hard work and community solidarity.

ADADO channels this global Kamba network into a focused development force, connecting the diaspora's resources with the homeland's needs — building the Kamba future together.
